    About Us

    Yanapa Mishi was founded with the primary goal of helping cats. In many South American countries, stray cats are abundant, and most of them suffer maltreatment. Because of this, we created a safe haven for stray cats where we provide rehabilitation in case they have any kind of wound or issue. Then, we seek a family for them so they can enjoy the loving life they deserve.
